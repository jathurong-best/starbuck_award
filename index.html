LEVERAGING BRAZE

SCALING PERSONALIZATION THROUGH
AUTOMATION

Following the success of the pilot CRM campaign, Braze was integrated to scale personalization. Automation enabled real-time engagement while streamlining operations and reducing manual workload.

Automated
Personalization
at Scale

Braze powered the automation of customer journey flows, enabling timely, relevant messages to each segment. This approach delivered personalized experiences efficiently and at scale.

SEAMLESS
CROSS-CHANNEL EXPERIENCE

App and email channels were connected through Braze, enhancing engagement and boosting retention through timely, contextual communication.
